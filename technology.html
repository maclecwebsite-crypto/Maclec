The World's First Commercially Deployable Dam-Free Hydropower Platform

Traditional hydropower depends upon dams, reservoirs, large civil structures and significant environmental intervention. MACLEC's Surface Hydrokinetic Turbine Technology generates renewable electricity directly from the natural kinetic energy of flowing water — without altering the river, canal or waterway.

Six Technology Advantages

Built Different. Engineered to Scale.

Every advantage below is a direct result of removing the dam, reservoir and major civil works from the hydropower equation.

01

No Dam Required

Generate electricity directly from flowing water — the canal or river keeps moving exactly as it always has.

02

24×7 Baseload Power

Unlike solar and wind, flowing water never stops — continuous energy generation through day and night.

03

Ultra Low Environmental Impact

No submergence. No displacement. No reservoir. Fish and aquatic life pass freely through every installation.

04

Plug & Play Installation

Factory manufactured modules arrive site-ready — rapid deployment with minimal civil works.

05

Modular & Scalable

Deploy from 50 kW → 100 kW → 250 kW → MW → GW by simply adding more turbine clusters.

06

40+ Year Design Life

Engineered for long-term, reliable operation under harsh field conditions with minimal maintenance.
