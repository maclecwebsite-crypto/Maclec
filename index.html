Existing Water Infrastructure

MACLEC's Surface Hydrokinetic Turbine technology enables clean, continuous and scalable electricity generation from naturally flowing or man-made water streams without dams, reservoirs, diversion structures or major civil construction.

Irrigation Canals

Round-the-clock renewable energy from existing canal networks spanning thousands of kilometres.

  • 24×7 power generation
  • No obstruction to irrigation flow
  • 50 kW to GW-scale deployment

  • No major civil modification
  • Hydro PSU and utility compatible
